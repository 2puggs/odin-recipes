MAC n CHEESE

Description

Mac & Cheese is a crowd favorite and there are many ways to make it. The way I propose is through a thick roux and baking more cheese in between layers of the shells covered in rich sauce. We don't skimp on butter or love here

Ingredients

    8 oz Colby Jack
    8 oz Sharp Cheddar
    Corkscrew Pasta
    Garlic Powder
    Salt
    Fresh Cracked Black Pepper
    Smoked Paprika
    All Purpose Flour
    Butter
    Milk

Steps

  1. Pre-head oven to 350 degrees F and butter a larger casserole dish.
  2. Cook noodles just shy of al dente. Drain well and do not rinse.
  3. In a new pot, melt the butter until foaming, slowly whisk in flower until bubbling.
  4. Whisk in milk, seasonings, and bring to boil. Remove from head and stir in half of combined shredded cheeses until melted.
  5. Add in noodles until combined, season with salt to desired taste. Pour into casserole dish. Layer with remaining cheese on top.
  6. Bake 25- 30 min. Broil for 2 min until crust forms.
